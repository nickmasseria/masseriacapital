Investment criteria

Business type
Any profitable SMB — services, light manufacturing, distribution, or niche B2B. We're industry-agnostic and follow the fundamentals.
Financial profile
$500K–$5M EBITDA with consistent profitability. We don't require explosive growth — stable and durable cash flow matters more.
Geography
Headquartered in California. We will be hands-on operators committed to the local community around the business.
Team & culture
A loyal team and a positive culture. We acquire businesses to steward them — protecting employees and customers is non-negotiable for us.
Transition readiness
Owners seeking retirement or a clean exit. We offer a respectful, flexible process and patience — no rushed timelines.
Defensibility
A real competitive moat — customer relationships, local reputation, recurring revenue, or operational know-how that's hard to replicate.

Our approach

"We're looking for a business to run, not a check to write."

Masseria Capital was founded on a simple belief: great small businesses deserve great stewards. Too many profitable, well-run companies end up fragmented, under-invested, or stripped of the culture that made them successful in the first place.

We're different. We are acquiring a single business — one we will personally manage and commit our careers to. When you work with us, you work directly with the decision-maker from day one through close.

The name masseria comes from the traditional Apulian farmstead — a self-sufficient estate built on hard work, community, and care for the land across generations. That ethos shapes how we think about business ownership.

We're patient capital. We're not beholden to fund timelines or return hurdles that force short-sighted decisions. Our incentive is simple: build something durable, take care of the people involved, and grow it for the long haul.

If you're thinking about a transition — even if it's years away — we'd love to have a confidential conversation.
